OpenGIN

OpenGIN is the core framework that power Lanka Data Foundation's entire ecosystem. It handles the collection, organization, and delivery of Sri Lanka's public data, ensuring that information is systematically archived, connected, and made accessible. This provides the foundation on which all our data services and applications are built.
