Who We Are & Why We Do This

Dedicated to creating transparency and accountability through accessible public information for everyone.

Our Story

Founded with the belief that transparency is the cornerstone of democracy, we started as a small group of technologists, activists, and citizens who recognized the gap between public information and public access. Our journey began when we realized that while government data exists, it often remains locked away in formats that are difficult for ordinary citizens to understand and use.

Today, we've grown into a dedicated team working tirelessly to bridge this gap, making public data not just available, but truly accessible and actionable for communities across the nation.

Transparency

We believe every citizen deserves access to clear, understandable information about how their government operates and spends public resources.

Empowerment

By making data accessible, we empower communities to make informed decisions, hold institutions accountable, and drive positive change.

Impact

Our work creates lasting impact by fostering a culture of openness, accountability, and civic engagement in our society.
