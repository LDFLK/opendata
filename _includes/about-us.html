Who We Are & Why We Do This

Dedicated to creating transparency and accountability through accessible public information for everyone.

Our Story

Founded with the belief that transparency is the cornerstone of democracy, we started as a small group of technologists, activists, and citizens who recognized the gap between public information and public access. Our journey began when we realized that while government data exists, it often remains locked away in formats that are difficult for ordinary citizens to understand and use.

Today, we've grown into a dedicated team working tirelessly to bridge this gap, making public data not just available, but truly accessible and actionable for communities across the nation.

Why We Do This

We believe that every citizen deserves access to clear, understandable information about how their government operates and spends public resources. Transparency isn't just about making data availableâ€”it's about making it meaningful and actionable for the people it affects most.

By empowering communities with accessible data, we foster a culture of accountability and civic engagement. Our work creates lasting impact by enabling informed decision-making, encouraging public participation, and building trust between citizens and institutions. When people have the tools to understand and engage with public information, democracy becomes stronger and more responsive to the needs of all.
