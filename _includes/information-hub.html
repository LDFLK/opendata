Information Hub

The Information Hub is the central access point for the public data we collect in Sri Lanka. It makes these datasets openly accessible together with applications that allow users to browse, search, and visualize the information. By consolidating fragmented information, the Data Hub ensures public data is easier to access and understand.

Data Archive

Our Datasets offer the raw public data we collect, available in formats including PDFs, CSVs, and JSONs. They can be freely downloaded, viewed, and reused.
