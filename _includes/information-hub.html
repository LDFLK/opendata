Sri Lanka Information Hub

The Sri Lanka Information Hub is the central access point for the public data we collect in Sri Lanka. It makes these datasets openly accessible together with applications that allow users to browse, search, and visualize the information. By consolidating fragmented information, the Data Hub ensures public data is easier to access and understand.

Data Explorer

The Data Explorer offers applications to navigate, visualize, and interact with the public datasets we collect, making it easier to understand and explore the information.
